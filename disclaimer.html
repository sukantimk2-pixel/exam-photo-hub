Accuracy of Exam Specifications

Smart Exam & Document Photo Hub maintains a database of photo and signature size requirements for Indian competitive exams and government documents. While we make every effort to keep this database accurate and current, we cannot guarantee that the specifications listed are always the latest or most accurate.

Exam-conducting bodies such as UPSC, SSC, IBPS, NTA, and state PSC commissions frequently update their requirements with each new examination notification. The specifications in our database may lag behind such updates.

Official Source Verification Required

Before submitting any photo or document for an official examination or government process, you must verify the required specifications on the official website of the relevant exam authority. Relying solely on our tool for exam submission without verification is done entirely at your own risk.

  • UPSC: upsc.gov.in
  • SSC: ssc.nic.in
  • IBPS: ibps.in
  • NTA (JEE/NEET/CUET): nta.ac.in
  • Railway RRB: indianrailways.gov.in
  • State PSC: Visit your respective state's Public Service Commission website
