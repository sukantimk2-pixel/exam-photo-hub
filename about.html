About Smart Exam Photo Hub

India's most comprehensive free tool for exam photo and document resizing.

Last updated: January 2026

Who We Are

Smart Exam & Document Photo Hub is a free, browser-based tool built specifically for Indian students, job seekers, and citizens who need to resize, edit, and format photos for government exams and official documents. We understand the stress of preparing for competitive exams — the last thing you should worry about is getting your photo size wrong.

This tool was built out of a genuine need. Thousands of Indian students lose exam opportunities simply because their uploaded photo or signature doesn't meet the exact pixel dimensions or file size requirements. We built this tool to eliminate that problem — forever, and at zero cost.

What We Offer

  • Photo and signature resizing for 100+ Indian exams — UPSC, SSC, RRB, IBPS, SBI, NEET, JEE, CUET, CTET, and all state PSC exams
  • Automatic background removal and color replacement (white, blue, grey, custom)
  • Brightness, contrast, saturation, and sharpness adjustments
  • Auto compression to meet official KB size limits
  • PDF tools: OCR text extraction, compression, splitting, rotating
  • Image → PDF and PDF → Image conversion
  • Merge multiple images and PDFs into a single document
  • Support for all Indian government documents: Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, Voter ID, and more

Our Principles

  • Free Forever: All tools are and will always remain completely free.
  • No Login Required: Use any feature without creating an account.
  • Privacy First: All image processing happens in your browser. No photos are ever sent to our servers.
  • Mobile-First: Designed to work perfectly on smartphones — because most Indian students use mobile devices.
  • Accuracy: Our database is maintained with official exam specifications. We review and update it regularly.

Data Security

We take your privacy extremely seriously. Every single image and PDF you upload is processed entirely within your browser using JavaScript. Your files are never transmitted to any server. Once you close or refresh the page, all data is immediately cleared from memory. We do not use any form of local storage for your files.

Important Disclaimer

While we maintain our exam database with care, photo specifications can change with each exam notification. Always verify the official photo requirements on the respective exam authority's website before submitting your application. This tool is a convenience aid, not a replacement for official guidelines.

Technology

This application is built using standard web technologies: HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. PDF functionality is powered by open-source libraries jsPDF and PDF.js, both licensed under the Apache 2.0 License. All other code is original and belongs to the platform owner.
