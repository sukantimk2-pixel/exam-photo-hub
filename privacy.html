Privacy Policy

We believe in radical transparency. Here's exactly what we do — and don't do — with your data.

Last updated: January 1, 2026

🔒 TL;DR: Your images and PDFs are processed entirely in your browser. We never see them, never store them, never transmit them. Zero.

1. Information We Collect

Images and Files: When you upload a photo, signature, or PDF to our tool, it is loaded directly into your browser's memory using the HTML5 File API and processed using JavaScript running locally on your device. These files are never uploaded to our servers. We have no access to them whatsoever.

Usage Analytics: We may use privacy-respecting analytics (such as Google Analytics with IP anonymization enabled) to understand how visitors use the site — which pages they visit, how long they stay, and what devices they use. This data is aggregated and never tied to personally identifiable information.

Contact Form: If you fill out our contact form, we collect your name, email address, and message content solely to respond to your inquiry. This information is not shared with third parties.

Cookies: We use essential cookies required for website functionality. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising profiling. Google AdSense, if enabled, may place cookies as per Google's own privacy policy.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve our tools and services
  • To respond to contact form submissions
  • To understand aggregate usage patterns and improve user experience
  • To serve relevant advertisements via Google AdSense (advertisers cannot identify you)

3. Data Storage and Security

Since files are processed in-browser, they are stored temporarily in your device's RAM only. When you close the browser tab or refresh the page, this data is immediately cleared. We use HTTPS encryption for all page content delivery. Our website does not have a database that stores user-uploaded files.

4. Third-Party Services

Google AdSense: We display advertisements powered by Google AdSense. Google may collect data as per their own Privacy Policy and use cookies to serve personalized ads. You can opt out via Google's Ad Settings.

Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics with anonymized IPs to measure traffic. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

CDN Libraries: We load PDF.js and jsPDF from Cloudflare CDN. These services may log your IP address for security purposes as per their own policies.

5. Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for students preparing for competitive exams and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted personal information to us, please contact us immediately.

6. Your Rights

  • Right to access: You can request what data we hold about you (contact form submissions only)
  • Right to deletion: You can request deletion of your contact form data at any time
  • Right to opt out of analytics: Use browser settings or opt-out tools described above

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be reflected with an updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

For any privacy-related concerns, please contact us via our Contact Page or email us at privacy@examphoto.in.
